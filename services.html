Our Services

Discover a world of comprehensive veterinary services at Akira Vet. Our commitment goes beyond treating ailments; we focus on preventive care to ensure a lifetime of well-being for your pets.

Explore our diverse range of services designed to meet the unique needs of every pet, delivered with the warmth and compassion they deserve.

Call Us for Assistance or to Book an Appointment

Rutine Checks

Regular check-ups are the foundation of your pet's health. Our routine checks cover comprehensive examinations, ensuring early detection of potential issues and promoting a proactive approach to your pet's well-being.

Vaccination

Protect your pet from preventable diseases with our tailored vaccination programs. We prioritize preventive care to keep your furry friends healthy and happy, providing vaccinations that align with their individual needs.

Surgery

In times of medical necessity, trust Akira Vet for safe and effective surgical interventions. Our skilled veterinary surgeons employ the latest techniques and equipment to ensure optimal outcomes for your pet.

Grooming

Pamper your pets with our grooming services, designed to enhance their well-being and keep them looking and feeling their best. From a soothing bath to a stylish trim, our grooming experts cater to your pet's individual needs.

Pet Daycare

Give your pet a day of fun and socialization at our pet daycare facility. Our supervised and engaging environment provides a safe space for pets to play, exercise, and enjoy the company of fellow furry friends.

Pet Store

Explore our pet store for quality products that cater to the unique needs of your pets. From premium pet food to toys and accessories, our curated selection ensures your pets receive the best care and attention.
